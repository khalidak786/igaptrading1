About IGAP Trading

Two decades of excellence in global trading and supply chain solutions

Our Story

Founded in the early 2000s, IGAP Trading has grown from a regional trading company to a global player with offices in the UAE, UK, Canada, India, and Pakistan. Our journey has been marked by consistent growth, adaptability, and a commitment to excellence in every transaction.

With over 20 years of experience in finance, logistics, manufacturing, and trading, we've developed a unique understanding of the complete trade cycle from raw material procurement to final product delivery.

Our Mission

To connect global markets through efficient, reliable, and sustainable trading solutions that create value for all stakeholders. We aim to be the preferred partner for businesses looking for end-to-end trade solutions with a focus on quality, reliability, and environmental responsibility.

Our mission extends beyond transactions - we're committed to building long-term relationships and contributing to sustainable global trade practices.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We conduct business with honesty and transparency in all our dealings.
  • Expertise: Two decades of experience informs every decision we make.
  • Reliability: Our clients trust us to deliver on our promises, every time.
  • Sustainability: We promote environmentally responsible trading practices.
  • Innovation: We continuously adapt to changing market dynamics and technologies.
